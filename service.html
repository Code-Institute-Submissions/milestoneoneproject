IoT

IoT (the Internet of things) is no longer just a buzzword. We live in a world where connectivity is ubiquitous and everything from industry, business through home is becoming “smart”. Your ability to leverage connected devices with real-time analytics and machine learning will make or break your business. bSentient provides experienced consultants and developers that can help you make the transition from a non-connected product-centric business to a connected smart solutions business.

Functional prototypes

Robust functional prototypes can help you perform early user-testing, convince stakeholders or just impress at trade fairs. Such prototypes trade up-front investment, feature completeness, manufacturability and maintainability for a substantially shortened lead-time. At bSentient we have ample experience providing robust functional prototypes that meet your criteria.

Open-source

After more than a decade of misinformation fear, open-source has won. You need to adopt open-source software in your products and development process to remain competitive. But the adoption is not free: Where open-source software is free to use and distribute, your business will have to comply with the legal obligations stipulated by various software licenses. The cost of adoption however is insignificant compared to the benefit. Let us guide you in the safe adoption of open-source software: We are experienced at battling misinformation, training management and developers alike, and helping you put healthy open-source processes in place.
